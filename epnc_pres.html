Workshop Overview & National Impact

This transformative national professional development initiative brought together educators, academic advisors, and staff from NCAE-C designated institutions and K-12 schools with clear pathways into NCAE-C colleges and universities. The workshop series focused on equipping participants with essential knowledge and practical tools to integrate Artificial Intelligence into their teaching practices and academic processes, directly supporting national cybersecurity and AI workforce development initiatives.

Machine Learning Integration

Practical applications in course design and student engagement strategies

Leveraging AI to enhance learning outcomes and institutional processes

Workforce Preparation

Preparing students for success in an AI-driven federal workforce
