Create a comprehensive, responsible use policy for artificial intelligence in your educational setting. Based on current best practices and research-backed guidelines.

Overview & Initial Setup

Welcome! This tool will guide you through creating a comprehensive AI policy for your classroom or institution. The process follows research-based best practices and includes stakeholder input, ethical considerations, and implementation planning.
