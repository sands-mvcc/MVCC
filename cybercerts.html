CompTIA Security+
CompTIA
Exam Cost: $392
Study Time: 1-3 months
Prerequisites: None (2 years IT experience recommended)
Difficulty: Beginner
Validity: 3 years
Average Salary: $70,000 - $90,000
Job Roles: Security Analyst, Systems Admin, IT Support

Pros:

  • Most popular entry-level certification
  • Vendor-neutral and globally recognized
  • DoD 8140 approved
  • Strong foundation for career growth
  • No prerequisites required

Cons:

  • Moderate cost compared to free alternatives
  • Requires renewal every 3 years
  • Covers broad topics, not deeply specialized
Certified in Cybersecurity (CC)
ISC2
Exam Cost: FREE (limited time)
Study Time: 1-2 months
Prerequisites: None
Difficulty: Beginner
Validity: Annual renewal
Average Salary: $65,000 - $85,000

  • Completely free exam and training

  • Access to ISC2 professional community

Cons:

  • Less industry recognition than Security+

Exam Cost: $2,499 (with training)

Prerequisites: Basic IT/networking knowledge
Difficulty: Intermediate
Validity: 4 years

  • Highly respected SANS training

Cons:

  • Very expensive ($2,500+)
  • More challenging than other entry-level certs
  • Requires significant time investment
  • May be overwhelming for true beginners
