⚠️ Best Practices & Ethics

  • ✅ Always review and customize AI-generated content
  • 🔒 Don't upload student personal information
  • 🎯 Use Claude as a teaching assistant, not a replacement
  • 📚 Encourage students to think critically about AI-generated content
  • 🌟 Share successful prompts and strategies with colleagues
