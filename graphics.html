# Type Description Example Use in Teaching

1Prepare the Lesson Content

Use your detailed lesson outline about Types of Business ownership, or refer to this structure:

  • Sole Proprietorship
  • Partnership
  • Corporation (C-Corp, S-Corp)
  • LLC
  • Cooperative
  • Franchise
  • Key comparison points (e.g., liability, taxation, ease of formation)

2Open ChatGPT and Use the Graphic Generation Prompt

In ChatGPT, use the following prompt to generate a visual handout:

Act as an instructional designer and advanced graphic generator. Create a high-quality, visually engaging handout for students in a business class. The topic is "Comparison of Types of Business Ownership in the U.S." Generate a handout design that includes: - A clear title and subtitle - A visual comparison chart or infographic comparing Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, Corporation, LLC, Cooperative, and Franchise - Key categories: Liability, Taxes, Formation Difficulty, Ownership, Example Businesses - Clean, modern formatting suitable for print or PDF - Business-relevant icons or illustrations to enhance visual appeal - Use bold headings, section dividers, and enough white space for readability Make this handout look like it belongs in a college-level business course.

3Review the Generated Handout

ChatGPT will return a photo-quality handout image (usually in PNG format). Review the image for:

  • Accuracy of the content
  • Clarity of layout and visual organization
  • Proper comparison categories
  • Readability (can students quickly interpret it?)

If needed, prompt:

  • "Please revise the graphic to simplify the text"
  • "Add icons for each business type to make it more engaging"

4Download and Use the Graphic

After reviewing:

  1. Click the download icon on the generated image
  2. Save the file as PNG or JPEG
  3. Insert the image into:
    • Google Docs or Word handouts
    • PDF worksheets
    • Google Slides or PowerPoint
    • Your LMS (Canvas, Blackboard, etc.)
    • Print for in-class distribution
